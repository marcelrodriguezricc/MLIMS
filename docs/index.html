Working Title

a compendium of thought, research, and creation by Marcel Rodriguez-Riccelli

This webpage serves as a latent space to collect abstractions of my work pertaining to the field of new media art. I'm an installation artist, musical performer, and technologist currently working as a graduate student with UC Santa Barbara's Media Arts & Technology department, particularly with the transLAB and Systemics Lab.
